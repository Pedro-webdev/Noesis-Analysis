About Us

Noesis Analysis is an independent consultancy dedicated to producing applied intelligence in contexts where uncertainty poses an operational, reputational or institutional risk.

Our approach combines political analysis, security assessment and scenario building, with the aim of providing public and private clients with tools to anticipate risks, understand complex dynamics and act with greater predictability.

We work according to criteria of analytical neutrality, methodological rigour and practical orientation:

  • The work carried out by Noesis Analysis is conducted under strict standards of discretion and confidentiality.
  • Information provided by clients is treated confidentially.
  • Reports are prepared in accordance with professional standards, with a preventive approach and analytical neutrality.

Services

Noesis Analysis offers applied intelligence solutions focused on risk prevention, monitoring critical variables, and building scenarios for strategic and operational decision-making.

Applied Intelligence for Institutional Security
